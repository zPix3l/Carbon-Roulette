# BigWater Games

Daily betting games built on real market data. Players spot fraud, verify numbers, and learn how climate markets actually work -- one bet at a time.

---

### Carbon Roulette
A fictional carbon credit project drops every day. Players analyze the methodology, the standard, the price, the yield -- and bet on whether it's legit or has integrity issues. 30 rounds of increasing difficulty, built on 4 verified datasets covering 10 carbon methods and 7 certification standards.
→ [Full overview](./game-overview.md)

### Water Roulette *(coming soon)*
Same format, applied to water credit markets. Gold Standard Water Benefit Certificates, BEF Water Restoration Certificates, WHO compliance, regional scarcity baselines. Coming after Carbon Roulette ships.
